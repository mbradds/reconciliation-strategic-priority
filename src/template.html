First Nations Section

Section updated April 2021

A significant amount of CER regualted pipelines and facilities reside within First Nations Reserves. Many of the CER's events, including incident review, conditions complaince, inspections, and complaince verification activities, among others rely on involvement from First Nations groups as a key stakeholder.

This section of the pipeline profiles highlights the intersections and proximity of CER regulated infrastructure and activities with First Nations Reserves, as well as historic treaty lands.

Reconciliation with Canada's First Nations communities is one of the CER's four strategic priorities. Learn more about how we engage with Indigenous Peoples here.
