Dashboard Instructions
  • Please use Chrome or Edge when viewing this prototype!
  • You may need to refresh the page or press F5 to view the most recent version of the prototype.
  • Use your mouse, trackpad, or the buttons in the upper left corner of the map to zoom in and out.
  • Click the "Reset Map" button to reset the map scope and sidebar.
  • The yellow highlighted blue regions correspond to First Nations reserves where an overlap with CER Infrastructure has been detected using an automated GIS process.
  • The calculations, distances, and totals are estimates based on the best available data and GIS assumptions. The accuracy of this map is not guaranteed, and the values/analysis are subject to change based on data availability and GIS assumptions.
