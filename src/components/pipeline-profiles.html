Pipeline Profile Section

Updated August 2021

A significant amount of CER regualted pipelines, facilities, and events reside within First Nations Reserves, Traditional Territories, and Treaty Lands. CER events and operations such as pipeline incident reviews, remediation monitoring, inspections, complaince verification activities, and others rely on Indigenous groups as a key stakeholder.

This pipeline profiles section and dashboard highlights the intersections and proximity of CER regulated infrastructure and activities with First Nations Reserves, as well as historic treaty lands.

Reconciliation with Canada's First Nations communities is one of the CER's four strategic priorities. Learn more about how we engage with Indigenous Peoples here.

We invite Indigenous communities to be more involved, not just in our hearings and oversight work, but also in our energy and pipeline information programs. Please reach out to Pipeline.Info@cer-rec.gc.ca for more information.
